Problem Statement

First-time homebuyers struggle to understand complex mortgage structures and assess long-term affordability, leading to financial uncertainty and potential miscalculations in home purchases.

The problem is that real estate is hard to understand especially the mortgages to a common person. I know this first hand because I am currently studying real estate and all of the different types of mortgages are confusing. This problem aims for a solution to make the process simplier or easier.
